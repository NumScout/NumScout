### experiment

The results of our conducted experiments.

95_Samples_Run: Small-scale experiments

- 95_samples.csv: A randomly sampled data set of 95 contracts with a confidence interval of 10 and a confidence level of 95%.
- 95_Pruning_Result: samples pruning results.
- 95_samples: Source code and experimental results of 95 samples.
- 95_samples.json: Statistics of experimental results of 95 samples.
- 95_labels.xlsx: label results of 95 samples.

Ablation_Experiment_Run: Ablation experiment

- ablation_experiment_res: Ablation experiment results of 95 samples.
- ablation_experiment.json: Ablation experiment results statistics.

Case: Poc of a detected real case.

Large_Scale_Run: Large-scale experiments of 6,617 contracts.

- large_dataset.csv: Large-scale dataset.
- large_dataset_without_95.csv: Large-scale dataset after removing 95 samples.
- Large_Pruning_Results.tar.gz: Large-scale dataset pruning results.
- Large_Scale_Dataset.tar.gz: Large-scale dataset source code.
- Large_Scale_Dataset_Res.tar.gz: Large-scale dataset experimental results.
- large.json: Statistics of experimental results of large-scale datasets.

Open_Card: 

- keywords.txt: Complete list of matching keywords.
- related_reports.csv: Audit reports filtered by keywords.
- related_reports_deduplication.csv: Audit report after deduplication.
- opencard_count.xlsx: Statistics on the severity of numerical defects in audit reports.
